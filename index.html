🔒 PRIVACY POLICY — April 2026

Data Stored on Your Device (Local Only)

All of the following data is stored only on your device in your browser. We never send this to our servers or any other company:

  • Weekly pick selections and confidence scores
  • Pool results (wins, losses, points each week)
  • Season performance history
  • Game notes and preferences

You control this data: Clear your browser cache anytime to delete everything permanently.

Weather & Lightning Data (Optional Features)

When you click "Load Weather," the app fetches real-time weather data from Open-Meteo (openmeteo.com) to help you adjust confidence scores for outdoor stadiums. We also provide a link to LightningMaps.org for real-time lightning tracking near game venues.

What we send:

  • Stadium latitude and longitude coordinates (public information)
  • NO personal data: No names, emails, device IDs, or personal identifiers

Your control:

  • Both features are completely optional — use them only when you need weather or lightning data
  • The app works 100% offline without these features
  • Not affiliated: We are not affiliated with Open-Meteo or LightningMaps. Review their privacy policies: openmeteo.com/en/privacy

What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone, address)
  • ❌ Account logins or authentication data
  • ❌ Device identifiers, tracking cookies, or analytics
  • ❌ Your actual location or location history
  • ❌ Betting or gambling activity
  • ❌ Crash reports or error logs

We have no servers, no account system, and no way to track you. All your data stays on your device.
