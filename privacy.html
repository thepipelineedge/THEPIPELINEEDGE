Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

Welcome to The Pipeline Edge. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Overview

The Pipeline Edge is an informational tool for confidence pool and pick'em players. We collect minimal data and prioritize your privacy by storing all information locally on your device.

2. Data We Collect

We do NOT collect personal data. The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Weekly picks (stored locally on your device)
  • Pool results and performance history
  • Game selections and confidence rankings

3. Automatic Data

  • App crash reports — sent to Google Play Services (Android) or Apple (iOS)
  • Basic usage analytics (no personal identifiers)

4. Optional Features

  • Email address (only if you create an account)
  • Cloud backup of your picks (if enabled)

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not store personal data on our servers
  • Optional account data is encrypted
  • We do NOT sell or share your data
  • Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS

6. Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics (usage only, no personal identifiers)
  • Apple and Google crash reporting services
  • Google Play Store (app distribution and purchases)
  • Cloudflare (web hosting)

7. Important Notice - Not a Betting App

This app does NOT:

  • Facilitate gambling or wagering
  • Connect to sportsbooks
  • Provide betting odds or advice
  • Promote real-money betting

This app is for informational and entertainment purposes only.

8. Your Rights

  • Delete your data at any time by clearing your browser cache or app storage
  • Request information about stored data
  • Opt out of analytics (if applicable)
  • Disable optional features anytime
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently received personal information from a child under 13, we will delete such information immediately.

Parents or guardians: If you believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us at thepipelineedge@gmail.com.

10. Refunds & Purchases

All refunds are managed exclusively by Google Play Store, not by The Pipeline Edge.

Refund Policy:

  • Refund window: 48 hours from purchase
  • To request a refund: Open Google Play Store > Manage Purchases > Select The Pipeline Edge > Request Refund
  • Google Play processes refunds per their standard policy
  • We cannot manually process refunds or extend the refund window
  • For refund issues, contact Google Play Store support directly

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with the new effective date. Your continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
